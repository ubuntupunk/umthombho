I-Netbones Iyazingca Ngokuxhasa U-Umthombhu

Inkcazo: uxhaso lubhengeziwe

Ukuvala Umahluko Wedijithali, Ibali Elinye Ngexesha



Kwi-Netbones South Africa, sikholelwa ukuba ubuchwepheshe bufanele buncede uluntu, hayi ngenye indlela. Yiyo loo nto sizingca kakhulu ngokubhengeza uxhaso lwethu lwe-Umthombho - intsebenziswano emele yonke into esiyimelayo: ukubandakanywa, ukufikeleleka, kunye namandla amazwi asekuhlaleni.

**Okungaphezulu Kweteknoloji Nje - Kuphathelele Abantu**



Umahluko wedijithali eMzantsi Afrika awukho nje malunga nokufikelela kwizixhobo okanye uqhagamshelo lwe-intanethi. Kumalunga nemiqobo yolwimi, ukumelwa kwenkcubeko, kunye nokuqinisekisa ukuba ubuchwepheshe bunika amandla wonke umntu - kungekuphela nje abo bathetha isiNgesi okanye abahlala kwiindawo zasezidolophini.



U-Umthombho uyakuqonda oku nzulu, kwaye ngoxhaso lwethu, sibabonelela ngenkqubo yolawulo lomxholo oluphakathi oludibanisa le miqobo ngeendlela ezinentsingiselo.



**Amandla okuPapasha okuPhakathi-OkuMbini**



Isisombululo sethu solawulo lomxholo kwi-Umthombo senza into ekhethekileyo: senza kube lula ukudala umxholo weelwimi ezininzi ngaxeshanye ngokulula okungazange kwenzeke ngaphambili.

**Ulwimi njengeBridge, hayi umqobo**



Kwilizwe elineelwimi ezili-11 ezisemthethweni, ukudala umxholo akufanele kunyanzele uluntu ukuba lukhethe phakathi kwesiNgesi nolwimi lwabo lwenkobe. Inkqubo yethu ivumela abadali bomxholo be-Umthombo ukuba:



Babhale ngokwendalo ngesiNgesi nangesiXhosa

Bagcine amazwi ayinyani kulwimi ngalunye ngaphandle kokuyekelela

Bafikelele kubaphulaphuli abaninzi ngokwenza umxholo ufikeleleke kuluntu olwahlukeneyo

Bagcine ulwazi lwenkcubeko oluhlala lulahleka kwiindlela zokuguqulela kuphela



Xa umntu eMpuma Koloni enokufunda ibali ngesiXhosa ngelixa umntu asebenza naye eGoli efunda ibali elifanayo ngesiNgesi - zombini ngomgangatho olinganayo kunye nobunyani benkcubeko - kulapho iteknoloji ibanceda ngokwenene abantu.

**Ukwenza iTekhnoloji ifikeleleke**



Iinkqubo zolawulo lomxholo wendabuko zihlala zakhiwa ngabaphuhlisi, kubaphuhlisi. Ziyaboyikisa, ziyinkimbinkimbi, kwaye zicinga ukuba zinezinga lolwazi lobuchwephesha olukhupha abantu abaninzi abanokuba ngabadali bomxholo. Inkqubo yethu yahlukile:



Ujongano oluqondayo olungadingi qeqesho lobuchwephesha

Iimboniso ezibonakalayo ukuze abadali babone kanye into abayipapashayo

Ukutshintsha ulwimi ngedrophu elula - akukho kudideka, akukho bunzima

Uyilo olugxile kuluntu oluhlonipha indlela abantu bokwenyani abasebenza ngayo



**Ukunika amandla amazwi asekuhlaleni**



Xa iteknoloji inzima kakhulu, ngabantu abanobuchule bobuchwephesha kuphela abafumana ithuba lokubalisa amabali. Xa ikwisiNgesi kuphela, ngabantu abathetha isiNgesi kuphela abaviwayo. Injongo ka-Umthombo kukukhulisa amazwi oluntu, kwaye i-CMS yethu iqinisekisa ukuba:



Iintatheli zoluntu zinokupapasha ngokuthe ngqo ngaphandle kwabalamli

Ukukhetha ulwimi kusoloko kufumaneka, akukaze kucingelwe kamva

Izikhewu zezakhono zedijithali azithulisi amabali abalulekileyo

Ubunyani benkcubeko bugcinwa kuzo zonke iilwimi



**Ingqwalasela yoLuntu: Iteknoloji Esebenzayo**



Kwi-Netbones, sikhethe ukwakha izisombululo zethu kwitekhnoloji evulelekileyo kuba sikholelwa kuphuhliso oluqhutywa luluntu. Kanye njengokuba i-Umthombo ikhonza uluntu lwayo ngokubalisa amabali, sikhonza ezethu ngetekhnoloji efikelelekayo necacileyo.



**Kutheni i-Open Source ibalulekile**



Akukho mthengisi uvalelekileyo - Uluntu luphethe umxholo walo kunye neqonga lalo

Ukucaca - Ungayibona ngqo indlela esebenza ngayo inkqubo

Intsebenziswano - Uphuculo luzuzisa wonke umntu, hayi inkampani enye kuphela

Uzinzo - Izisombululo ezihlala ixesha elide, ngaphandle kweentlawulo zelayisenisi ezibizayo



**Ukwakha Ixesha Elide**



Oku akusiyo nje inkxaso-mali - kukuzibophelela. Sityala imali ku:



Inkxaso yobugcisa eqhubekayo ukuqinisekisa ukuba iqonga lika-Umthombo lisebenza kakuhle

Uqeqesho kunye nokwakhiwa kwamandla ukuze iqela labo lizimele

Uphuculo oluqhubekayo olusekelwe kwiimfuno zoluntu zehlabathi lokwenyani

Izisombululo ezinokukhula njengoko ukufikelela kuka-Umthombo kukhula



**Impembelelo Esiyakhayo Kunye**



Xa u-Umthombo epapasha ibali ngesiNgesi nangesiXhosa, benza okungaphezulu kokuguqulela amagama. Zizinto ezilandelayo:



Ukuqinisekisa ukuba isiXhosa sibaluleke njengesiNgesi kwindawo yedijithali

Kubandakanya abafundi abanokuthi bashiywe ngaphandle kwiincoko ezibalulekileyo

Ukugcina ulwimi nenkcubeko kwihlabathi ledijithali elihambelana ngakumbi

Ukubonisa ukuba iteknoloji inokuhlonipha nokuphakamisa iilwimi zaseAfrika



**Umbono wethu wokubandakanywa kwedijithali**



Ukwahlukana kwedijithali akuyi kuvalwa ngokubonelela nje ngokufikelela kwi-intanethi okanye izixhobo ezingabizi kakhulu. Kuya kuvalwa xa:



Ulwimi alusenguwo umqobo ekudaleni nasekusebenziseni umxholo wedijithali

Ubunzima bobuchwephesha abubakhuseli ababalisi bamabali abanetalente

Amazwi oluntu anokufikelela kubaphulaphuli ngaphandle kwabalamli

Ukunyaniseka kwenkcubeko kugcinwa kuwo onke amaqonga edijithali
